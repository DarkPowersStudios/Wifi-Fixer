Phone Interface


On a phone, the screeen is divided into two sections:
  • Top
    • The top section contains the Service fragment.

  • Bottom
    • The bottom section is a panel which shows Known Networks     by default.
    You can drag left to reveal Local Networks     and Status.


    Tapping the menu button will reveal additional options.
    (Note: Android 4.0 devices will see some of the menu options in the upper right as icons)
