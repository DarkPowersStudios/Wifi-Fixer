Local Networks


Local Networks shows networks scanned in your vicinity.    This list is updated as scan data is received.

Several operations are available upon long-pressing a network in the list.  The options available will vary depending on which are possible.

  • Connect
    • Connection confirmation will launch.

  • Add
    • Fragment will launch allowing wifi key to be entered.  Once key is entered, network will appear in Known Networks    .

  • About
    • Fragment will launch displaying scanned information about selected network.


    Notes:

    During connection, Wifi Fixer may change the enabled state of your networks.  They will be restored after connect.

    Pre-Android 3.0, the Known Networks list cannot be populated if wifi is disabled.
