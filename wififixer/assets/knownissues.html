Known Issues:

Wifi Sleep Policy


On Nexus One devices and some few others, Wifi Sleep Policy is ignored when the screen is off.

This has been reported happening on every Android version, and may have to do with the specific wifi chipset.

There is currently no workaround other than setting Wifi Performance to Max Wifi. This may or may not work.


Network Notifications

Because Wifi Fixer intercepts Wifi scans, it causes Network Notifications to function erratically.

Wifi Fixer will have its own Network Notifications in a future release.


Slower Reconnection

The main network check was reduced from every 5 seconds to every 10 seconds. In testing, it was found that the perceived responsiveness was nearly as high as 5 seconds, but the battery savings were substantial.

Hidden SSIDs

Android is not able to work with wifi networks using hidden SSIDs.

If your network is set to non-broadcast SSID, you will have to change it to broadcast.


Task Killers

Important: Wifi Fixer's service can be rendered inoperable by task managers and auto task killers.

It is strongly recommended that you do not use Wifi Fixer with this kind of software, but if you must, add WifiFixerService and LogService to the allowed list.
