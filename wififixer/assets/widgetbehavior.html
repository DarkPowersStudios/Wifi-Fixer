Widget Behavior


A dialog is shown which selects the behavior when the widget is tapped on the home screen.

  • Reassociate
    • Reassociates with currently connected network

  • Reset Wifi
    • Toggles wifi off then back on. (Will reset a " stuck " supplicant)

  • Known Networks
    • Opens the main Wifi Fixer activity, sliding open the Known Networks panel
