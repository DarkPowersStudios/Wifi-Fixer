Tablet Interface


On a Tablet, the screen is divided into four sections:
  • Top Left
    • Contains the Service fragment.

  • Top Right
    • Contains Status by default.  Using the Local Networks menus will display fragments here.
    To return to the Status fragment, you may swipe left or use the back button to move back one fragment, or tap the app logo in the upper left hand corner to return to Status

  • Bottom Left
    • Contains the Known Networks fragment.

  • Bottom Right
    • Contains the     Local Networks fragment.

    On the upper right are the Preferences and Help icons.    In portrait the About icon will be hidden, but available by tapping the menu button.
