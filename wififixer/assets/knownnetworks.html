Known Networks


Known Networks shows networks you have connected to in the past.    This contents of this list are identical to the ones in your device's settings under Wifi Networks.

Known networks which are in range display with green text.

Network Icon:
  • Enabled/Managed networks are white.
  • Disabled networks are black.
  • Non-managed networks are blue.

Several operations are available upon long-pressing a network in the list.  The options available will vary depending on which are possible.

  • Enable
    • Network may be connected to.

  • Disable
    • Network may not be connected to.

  • Connect
    • Connect     to network immediately.

  • Toggle Non-Managed
    • Selects whether Wifi Fixer should do network checks and repairs on selected network.  

  • Remove
    • Permanently remove network from wifi settings.

    Notes:

    During connection, Wifi Fixer may change the enabled state of your networks.  They will be restored after connect.

    Pre-Android 3.0, the Known Networks list cannot be populated if wifi is disabled.
